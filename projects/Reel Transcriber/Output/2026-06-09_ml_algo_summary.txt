**Key Points:**

1. **Linear Regression**: Predicts continuous values (e.g., house prices) by drawing a straight line through data points.
2. **Logistic Regression**: Used for binary classification problems (e.g., loan approval) by calculating the probability of an event occurring and mapping it to 0-1 using the sigmoid function.
3. **Decision Trees**: Splits data sets into smaller subsets based on conditions until reaching a decision point, easy to visualize and explain.
4. **Support Vector Machines (SVM)**: Finds the optimal boundary between two categories of data points by maximizing the distance between them.
5. **K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN)**: Assigns a label to new data points based on the most common label among its closest K neighbors.
