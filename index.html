Full job description
Director, Data Science - Applied AI Walmart Global Tech ABOUT THE TEAM Applied AI focuses on the development and deployment of innovative agentic AI and Machine Learning algorithms across all aspects of Walmart's retail, grocery and logistics businesses. The team of more than 300 AI scientists and engineers use advanced Applied AI, Agentic AI, and Mathematical Optimization to revolutionize how Walmart manages its retail, grocery and pharmaceutical operations. WHAT YOU WILL DO - Lead and grow a team of data scientists and data engineers dedicated to forecasting and gen AI initiatives. - Partner with product, engineering, merchandising, and business stakeholders to identify opportunities and align on strategic initiatives. - Oversee the end-to-end data science lifecycle: problem scoping, literature review, model design, algorithmic optimization, engineering integration, deployment, monitoring, and impact measurement. - Drive AI innovation in forecasting, including experimentation with new models and algorithms to improve accuracy and efficiency. - Ensure robust model governance, reproducibility, and ethical AI practices. - Communicate complex technical concepts and business impact effectively to technical and non-technical stakeholders, including senior leadership. - Stay current with industry trends and emerging technologies in data science and AI. COMPENSATION Base salary: $169,000 - $338,000 Annual and quarterly performance bonuses Stock purchase plan 401k match, medical, vision, dental, paid parental leave, PTO HUBS Global HQ: Bentonville, AR Primary hubs: San Francisco Bay Area and New York/New Jersey
